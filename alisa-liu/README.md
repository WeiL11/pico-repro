# Alisa Liu — research reading notes

Undergraduate-friendly **reading notes on NLP researcher [Alisa Liu](https://alisawuffles.github.io/)** —
her research arc, signature papers (plain-English), full publication list, and discussion questions.

This is **instance #2** of an ongoing *self-driving research-and-teach* workflow (instance #1 is
[PICO](https://github.com/WeiL11/pico-repro)). The two pages here follow the project convention:

- **[`index.html`](index.html)** — the content (the reading notes).
- **[`methodology.html`](methodology.html)** — the method (how the notes were researched & built, with a
  workflow diagram), including the verify pass that caught a real misattribution before it shipped.

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step, no dependencies.

## Honesty notes

Built from her homepage + DBLP + ACL Anthology + arXiv (Google Scholar wasn't directly fetchable, so
per-paper citation counts are unverified). "Infini-gram" is **not** her paper (that's Jiacheng Liu);
DBLP conflates her with an unrelated HCI researcher of the same name. A few 2026 entries are preprints.
Every paper links to its primary source — please verify before citing.
