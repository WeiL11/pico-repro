How these notes were made — a fresh run

An independent, from-scratch application of our two-pass method to test that it reproduces.

← Content (the reading notes) · project methodology tracker

TL;DR

This direction was built by running our current method completely fresh — starting from only a Google Scholar URL, using no prior notes — to check the workflow reproduces on its own. It does: the agents independently re-identified the researcher, re-derived the arc, and re-verified every paper.

The method is the two-pass workflow under four principles: a broad parallel fan-out → a verify gate → one deep-read agent per paper → foldable, cited HTML, opening with a personal “what I find most interesting” take.

The workflow

  1. Explain the concept well — Intro (why) / Method (how) / Results / Discussion per paper, jargon defined.
  2. Align every statement with the research, with clear citations — primary sources only; flag the unverified.
  3. Every agent answers “what is the most important thing missing?” — a box per paper.
  4. Open with a personal “what I find most interesting” take.

What the fresh run independently established (and corrected)

Starting from only the Scholar URL, the agents:

  • Identified the researcher (Alisa Liu, UW; advisors Choi & Smith) and her arc, with no prior notes.
  • Disambiguated same-name people — an HCI “Alisa Liu” (stained-glass craft) and genomics / neuroscience authors are different people, excluded by field + co-author network.
  • Resolved authorship nuances from the papers themselves: SuperBPE and Data Mixture Inference are co-first (“equal contribution” in the PDFs); on Data Mixture Inference she is listed 2nd only because equal contributors are ordered alphabetically.
  • Re-verified every headline number against each paper’s own text (e.g. DExperts toxicity 0.520→0.128, proxy-tuning closing 88% of the gap, SuperBPE +4.0% at fixed compute).
The point of a from-scratch re-run is reproducibility: independent agents, reading only primary sources, arrived at the same picture — and tightened a few details. Compare with the earlier direction alisa-liu/.

Honest limitations

  • Google Scholar was not reliably fetchable; built from homepage + DBLP + ACL Anthology + arXiv, so per-paper citation counts are unverified (omitted).
  • A few 2026 entries are preprints; venues/titles may change. NVIDIA Nemotron-variant authorship at the per-paper level is unverified.
  • Deep reads were trusted from the per-paper agents’ primary-source checks; signature papers are well confirmed.
