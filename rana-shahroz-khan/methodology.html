What was new this run (v0.7)

  • The HTML generator did the assembly. The page was produced from rana-shahroz-khan/notes.py by tools/build_notes.py — no hand-writing of HTML. This cut the assembly time that dominated earlier runs.
  • No paywall thrashing. All four signature papers are arXiv-available, so Pass 2 ran ~3× faster than the previous (SIGCOMM-paywalled) direction. (The fail-fast-on-paywalls idea stays in the backlog for when it’s needed.)

What the critic + quality gate caught (and we fixed)

  • The unifying theme was implicit. The page now states it up front: “make LLMs cheap to adapt — and stress-test where adaptation and coordination break.”
  • CAR-LoRA is also first-author (the critic confirmed it, resolving an “unverified” flag) — now surfaced in the arc, list, and completeness box as the most recent node of the LoRA arc.
  • The optimal-transport / compression roots (PRANC, LOPT under Kolouri) are named, so the LoRA work doesn’t look like it appeared from nowhere.
  • Guardrails: the unrelated naval / ship-hull “Shahroz Khan” is flagged as a different person; citation counts are dated & marked approximate; Anthropic Fellow dates tightened.
The verify gate + quality gate again earned their keep: the selection held up, but the theme and a 5th first-author paper would have been missed without the critic.
