The workflow (v0.6)

What the critic + quality gate caught (and we fixed)

  • The unifying theme was implicit. The critic’s top finding: the real through-line is “augment, don’t replace — learn from what experts already built.” Now stated up front.
  • Author order is a landmine. Orca & DeepCC list Yen second (co-first); Sage lists him first (co-first). Verified and stated precisely — not flattened to “first author.”
  • ASMR is a different lab (Pinto/Chopra), not advisor Chao — now called out.
  • The 2014 graphics paper is most likely a namesake (absent from his CV) — demoted to “unverified.”
  • Paywalled SIGCOMM numbers (Orca, Sage) are flagged unverified rather than quoted.
This is the v0.6 difference: a dedicated pass asked “what’s missing / what’s most likely wrong?” before publishing, and its answers changed the page.

Provenance (per-claim sourcing)

Per the strengthened principle, claims carry a source from agent output to the final page. Each signature paper’s full deep-read is archived so every card is traceable:

  • _research/orca-sigcomm2020.md
  • _research/sage-sigcomm2023.md
  • _research/cellular-tcp-deepcc.md

Decisions (paper selection, disambiguation, budgets) are logged in DECISIONS.md.
